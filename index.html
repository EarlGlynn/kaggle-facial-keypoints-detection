Overview

The purpose of the Kaggle Facial Keypoints Detection project is to predict keypoint positions on images of human faces based on a training set of over 7000 images.

Based on the training set, the Kaggle competition asked for predictions of facial keypoints on a test set of nearly 1800 images.

A series of Jupyter notebooks was used to explore the Facial Keypoints Detection data and document the steps taken in a reproducible way.
